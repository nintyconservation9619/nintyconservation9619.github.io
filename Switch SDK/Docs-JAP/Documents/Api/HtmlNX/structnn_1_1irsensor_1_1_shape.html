詳解

Structure for storing shape information.

詳細
A shape is represented by its polygonal silhouette. This is a set of contiguous points stored in HandAnalysisSilhouetteState::points array. The order of these points tells whether the shape is filled or empty (e.g. a hole in another shape): the interior of the shape is always to the right of the oriented edges. The average and centroid of the intensity of the the camera frame inside the shape are also available.
